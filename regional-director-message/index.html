National Institute of open Schooling (NIOS) is a premiere Organisation which is working under the aegis of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) Govt. of India. It has undertaken long paces since 23rd November 1989, the date of its inception.

We are looking after the Education of the vast masses of the learners throughout the length and breadth of the Nation. Our vision is to provide sustainable inclusive learning with Universal and flexible access to quality School Education and skill development. Our mission is three fold.

  1. Providing relevant, continuing and holistic Education upto pre degree level through Open and Distance Learning system.
  2. Contributing to the universalisation of School Education.
  3. Catering to the Educational needs of the Prioritized target group for equity and social justice.
